Everything you need to know about TagThemAll Bot

How many WhatsApp accounts can I connect?

The number of accounts depends on your plan. Starter allows 5 sessions, Professional allows 25, Business allows 100, and Enterprise offers unlimited sessions.

Is my WhatsApp data secure?

Absolutely! We use end-to-end encryption and never store your messages. All data is processed locally and securely.

Can I use the bot for customer support?

Yes! TagThemAll Bot is perfect for customer support with features like automated responses, contact management, and broadcast messaging.

What happens if I exceed my session limit?

You can upgrade your plan anytime to increase your session limit. We'll notify you before you reach your current limit.

Do you offer custom integrations?

Yes! Enterprise customers get access to our API and can request custom integrations with their existing business tools.
